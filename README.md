# 📸 SlowFrame

**An anti-doomscrolling, Instax-inspired slow web experience.**

A tactile, intentional video player that respects your attention span and mental peace. No algorithms, no infinite scroll, no chaos—just wholesome content in a beautiful, camera-inspired interface.

## ✨ Features

- 🎨 **4 Aesthetic Themes**: Instax Classic, Coquette Pink, Beachy Blue, Floral Garden
- ⏱️ **Gentle Time Limits**: Set 5, 10, or 15-minute sessions
- 📷 **Tactile UI**: Turn the lens ring, pull the film lever, press the shutter
- 🌸 **Soft Stop**: When time's up, you're gently reminded to rest
- 🎞️ **Polaroid Player**: Videos presented like developing Instax photos

## 🎯 The Philosophy

Modern short-form video is designed to hijack your dopamine. SlowFrame is different:
- **No autoplay** – You choose when to watch next
- **No infinite scroll** – You set your time limit
- **No algorithms** – Only content you intentionally curate
- **Tactile friction** – Beautiful knobs and levers force mindful interaction

Built for the slow web movement. Made with 💗 for anyone tired of doomscrolling.
